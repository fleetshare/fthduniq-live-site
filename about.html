About FTH D-UNIQ

Structured support for building materials, procurement, logistics and project needs.

FTH D-UNIQ is a business support and coordination company focused on building materials supply, procurement support, logistics coordination, project support, and procurement record-keeping.

We help customers source materials, request quotes, coordinate delivery, and manage project-related needs with clearer communication, reliable follow-up, and better structure across Lagos and selected nearby locations.

What We Do

Practical support for customers who need structure, follow-up and coordination.

FTH D-UNIQ helps customers reduce the stress of scattered suppliers, unclear pricing, poor follow-up, and logistics confusion by creating a more organized process around material sourcing and project support.

Building Materials Supply

We support customers with sourcing and supply coordination for cement, blocks, sand, granite, interlocking stones, kerbs, and other related materials.

Procurement Support

We help customers review material needs, compare available options, follow up with suppliers, and coordinate procurement requests.

Logistics Coordination

We support delivery planning, truck coordination, site delivery follow-up, and customer updates where logistics support is required.

Project Support

We assist busy clients, homeowners, contractors, and project owners with structured follow-up for material and coordination needs.

Procurement Records & Expense Tracking

We help clients organize material purchase records, supplier invoices, delivery costs, and weekly expense summaries for better project accountability.

Customer Follow-Up

We help customers maintain clearer communication around requests, pending quotes, supplier updates, delivery timelines, and next steps.

Vision

Trusted coordination partner.

To become a trusted coordination partner for building materials supply, procurement support, logistics coordination, and project-support services across Lagos and selected nearby locations.

Mission

Simpler sourcing and support.

To simplify the process of sourcing, procuring, and coordinating building materials by providing structured communication, supplier follow-up, logistics support, and customer-focused service.

Value Proposition

Less stress. More structure.

FTH D-UNIQ reduces the stress of dealing with scattered suppliers, unclear pricing, delayed responses, and logistics confusion by helping customers make better-informed procurement and delivery decisions.
