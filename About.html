About FTH D-UNIQ

FTH D-UNIQ is a business support and coordination brand focused on building materials supply, procurement support, logistics coordination, and project-support services for clients across Lagos and selected nearby locations.

Company Overview

Structured support for materials, procurement and delivery needs.

We help customers reduce the stress of sourcing materials, speaking with multiple suppliers, arranging trucks, confirming delivery details, and following up on project-related requests. Our role is to bring better structure, clearer communication, and practical coordination into the building materials and project-support process.

What We Do

FTH D-UNIQ supports customers from request to material coordination and delivery follow-up.

Building Materials Supply

We support requests for cement, blocks, sand, granite, interlocking stones, kerbs, and related building materials based on availability, quantity, and location.

Procurement Support

We assist with supplier communication, price review, sourcing follow-up, material request handling, and structured procurement coordination.

Logistics Coordination

We help coordinate truck movement, delivery timing, site access, dispatch follow-up, and delivery-related communication.

Vision

To become a trusted coordination partner for building materials supply, procurement support, logistics coordination, and project-support services across Lagos and selected nearby locations.

Mission

To simplify the process of sourcing, procuring, and coordinating building materials by providing structured communication, supplier follow-up, logistics support, and customer-focused assistance.

Value Proposition

FTH D-UNIQ helps customers make informed procurement and delivery decisions while reducing the stress of scattered suppliers, unclear pricing, and logistics confusion.

Project Support

We support project owners with practical follow-up, vendor communication, delivery checks, coordination updates, and execution support where required.

Who We Serve

Customers who need reliable support without unnecessary confusion.

We serve busy professionals, homeowners, remote property owners, diaspora clients, contractors, site teams, and project owners who need better coordination for materials, procurement, logistics, and project support.
