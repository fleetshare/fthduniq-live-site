Questions Before You Request a Quote

Quick answers to help you understand our material sourcing, procurement, delivery, and project-support process.

What materials can FTH D-UNIQ help source?

We can support requests for cement, blocks, sand, granite, interlocking stones, kerbs, and other construction-related materials, depending on availability and location.

Do you sell directly or coordinate procurement?

FTH D-UNIQ supports material sourcing, procurement coordination, supplier follow-up, and delivery-related support. Some requests may be fulfilled through verified suppliers or partner channels.

How do I request a quote?

Complete the quote/request form with the material needed, quantity, delivery location, preferred timeline, and any special instructions.

Do you handle delivery?

Yes. Delivery coordination can be arranged depending on the material, quantity, truck availability, site access, and delivery location.

What areas do you serve?

FTH D-UNIQ currently serves Lagos and selected nearby locations. Expanded sourcing support may be available on request.

Can you support customers who are not physically available?

Yes. We support busy, remote, and diaspora clients who need structured follow-up for material sourcing, procurement, delivery, and project-related requests.

Can I request procurement record-keeping support?

Yes. We can help organize material purchase records, supplier invoices, delivery costs, and weekly procurement summaries. This is not tax accounting, audit, or financial advisory.

Why should I use the quote form instead of only calling?

The quote form helps us capture the correct details, including material type, quantity, location, timeline, and site contact, so we can review your request properly.

Need building materials, procurement support or logistics coordination?

Submit your material, quantity, location, and delivery details through the quote form so we can review your request properly.
